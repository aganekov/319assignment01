Iowa State University
Iowa State University is a leader in software innovation, offering cutting-edge programs in computer science, software engineering, and cybersecurity. With state-of-the-art research labs, hands-on learning experiences, and strong industry connections, students gain the skills needed to excel in the fast-paced tech world. Whether developing AI solutions or working on real-world applications, Cyclones graduate ready to lead in the software industry.

University of Illinois
The University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign is home to one of the top-ranked computer science and software engineering programs in the country. With a strong emphasis on artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and cybersecurity, UIUC provides students with access to groundbreaking research and collaborations with leading tech companies. Graduates leave with the expertise and connections to drive innovation in the software industry.

University of Kansas
The University of Kansas prepares students for success in software development with top-tier programs in computer science, information systems, and software engineering. KU offers hands-on experience in areas like mobile app development, cloud computing, and cybersecurity. With strong industry partnerships and a collaborative learning environment, Jayhawks graduate with the skills to shape the future of technology.

Indiana University Bloomington
Indiana University Bloomington offers a forward-thinking approach to software development through its renowned informatics, computer science, and data science programs. With hands-on learning in fields like game design, cybersecurity, and AI, IU equips students with the tools to build the next generation of technology. Hoosiers gain industry experience and career-ready skills to make an impact in the software world.
